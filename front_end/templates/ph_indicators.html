The water hardness indicates how hard or soft a water. The more calcium and magnesium contained in a water, the harder it is.

Hard water can cause lime stains in kitchen and bathroom. Because of its high calcium and magnesium content, it is healthier than soft water.
(Calcium: structure of bones and teeth, magnesium: building muscles and nerves)
